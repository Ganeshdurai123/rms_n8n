Welcome to the Request Management System (RMS)! This guide will help you navigate the application and make the most of its automation features.

---

## 1. Getting Started: The Dashboard
When you log in, you'll see your **Main Dashboard**.
- **Sidebar**: Quick access to Programs, Reports, and User Management.
- **Top Bar**: Search functionality and the **Notification Bell** (look for the "red dot" for alerts!).
- **Calendar/Sheet View**: Switch between visual deadlines or a spreadsheet-style overview.

---

## 2. Managing Programs
Programs are the "containers" for your work.
- **Browse Programs**: View all active programs you belong to.
- **Fields**: Each program has specific fields (Drop-downs, Checkboxes, Dates) tailored to its workflow.

---

## 3. Working with Requests
Requests are individual tickets or tasks within a program.
- **Creating a Request**: Click "New Request" within a program. Ensure you set a **Due Date** and **Assignee**.
- **Request Details**: Click any request to view its full history, comments, and current status.
- **Status Updates**: Change status (e.g., from `Submitted` to `In Review`) to move work forward.
- **Reminders**: If your request is nearing its deadline, n8n will automatically email you a notification!

---

## 4. Automation & Notifications
The system stays in sync using background automation:
- **In-App Notifications**: Click the bell icon ðŸ”” at the top to see recent updates, overdue alerts, and report completions.
- **Email Alerts**: Check your inbox for daily morning reminders (sent at 8:00 AM) for any upcoming or overdue deadlines.

---

## 5. Generating Reports
Need data? Use the **Reports** module to get insights:
- **Request a Report**: Go to the "Reports" page and click "Generate New Report".
- **Types of Reports**:
  - **Summary**: Overall stats on status and volume.
  - **Program**: Detailed breakdown of a specific program's field data.
  - **Overdue**: A targeted list of items past their due dates.
- **The Wait**: Once requested, you'll see a "Processing" status. When the bell icon rings, your report is ready to download!

---

## 6. Quick Tips
- **Search**: Use the global search to find requests by their title or ID.
- **Comments**: Use the comment section inside requests to collaborate with your team.
- **Direct Links**: Email reminders contain a "View Request" button that takes you straight to the task.
